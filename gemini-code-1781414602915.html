🧠 How the Spaced Repetition Engine Functions

This app implements a custom, highly optimized variation of the classic SuperMemo SM-2 algorithmic model. Instead of forcing you to memorize microscopic facts via digital flashcards, it tracks the mathematical decay of whole high-yield textbook topics or question blocks.

  • The Forgetting Curve Calculation: Every time you log a topic, the app tracks its repetition number and an internal Ease Factor (EF) (defaulting to 2.5). When you click a rating button, the algorithm calculates the exact day the topic returns.
  • The Optimized Intervallic Step: To prevent heavy backlogs from overwhelming you during clinical shifts, the early intervals are optimized into a tight clinical track:
    • Forgot: Reset to Day 1 review loop.
    • Hard: Schedules for next review in 3 days.
    • Good: Schedules for next review in 7 days.
    • Easy: Multiplies interval by the custom Ease Factor (pushes past 14 to 28+ days cleanly).
  • Adaptive Ease Adjustment: If you consistently mark a chapter as "Hard", the system dynamically lowers that topic's ease factor so it shows up more frequently until mastered.

🚀 High-Yield Strategy for NEET PG & INICET Aspirants

Internship Workflow Preservation

Typing thousands of digital flashcards while handling ward duty or structural post-duty calls is impossible. This tool operates as an administrative task manager for your physical ecosystem (e.g., KDT, Robbins, Bailey & Love, or your hand-annotated coaching notes).

How to Track Q-Bank Errors

When solving standard MCQ modules, don't waste precious time digitizing explanations. Instead, drop an entry under the MCQ Workout toggle with a clear marker—for example: GT 14 - Neuro-Ophthalmology Errors or Systemic Pharmacology Q-Bank Block 4. When it populates your queue, rapidly slide open your notebook to review your custom error log or custom high-yield tables.

Maintaining the Strategic 50/50 Metric

Look at the New vs Revised Today counter in the dashboard every morning. To achieve elite scores, you must balance raw learning with absolute consolidation. If the app displays 8 items due for revision (R), use that number to cap your daily workload by choosing to look at exactly 8 new topics (N) from your master physical syllabus. Enforcing this balance stops backlogs from building up when ward shifts get chaotic.

Data Portability Guarantee

Because this app runs entirely offline using your browser's secure localStorage, your data remains fully sandboxed. Use the Export Local Backup feature regularly to generate a lightweight text file, which you can import on any phone or tablet to keep your schedules synced across devices without relying on third-party cloud connections.
